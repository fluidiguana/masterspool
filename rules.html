Masters Golf Betting Pool Rules

Competition Mechanics

2024 Dates: Thursday April 11, 2024, through Sunday April 14, 2024

Your submission is due Thursday Morning prior to the start of the tournament at 6am Pacific. The moment the first player tees off, no further roster changes will be allowed. As soon as possible, everyone's draft results will be published on this site and the competition will commence!

Betting Pool

Your team is formed by drafting 10 golfers, who are spread across 6 pools according to their player ranking:

Pool Number of Golfers Number of Golfers selected by bettors Player Rank Example 2018 team
A 5 2 Best 5 Jordan Spieth, Tiger Woods
B 8 2 Next 8 Justin Rose, Bubba Watson
C (total - 13)/4 2 Next n Matt Kuchar, Adam Scott
D (total - 13)/4 2 Next n Patton Kizzire, SiWoo Kim
E (total - 13)/4 1 Next n Ryan Moore
F (total - 13)/4, rounding if necessary 1 Last n Doc Redman

Be sure to not draft golfers who are not actually playing. I do my best to remove them from the pool but sometimes late drops happen. Any change requests must be made electronically prior to the first tee-off to be considered. As an ironclad rule: Rosters will not be changed after the first tee-off.

Team Scoring:

  • Of the 10 golfers you'll choose to be on your team, the best 4 count. If golfers on your team are cut, that's just tough, so choose well especially in the lower pools.
  • For example: Team A's golfers are all -1 for the day. Team B's golfer best golfer shot -6, but the rest of the team shot +1. Team A's score is -4 (-1, -1, -1, -1), whereas Team B's score is -3 (-6, +1, +1, +1). Team A would have the superior score. The other 6 golfers on each team are irrelevant for that scoring session.
  • The golfers that count can change day-to-day, and for the overall.

Payouts:

  • Day winners (4): 10% each
  • Overall 2nd place: 20%
  • Overall 1st place: 40%
